FreeLandCo Privacy and Policy

FreelandCo.com is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our website.

Information we collect

When you visit our website, we may collect the following information:

● Personal information you provide to us, such as your name, email address, and phone number.

● Information about your property, such as its address and your mortgage status.

● Usage data, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information.

● Cookies and similar technologies, which allow us to recognize you and customize your experience.

How we use your information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

● To provide and improve our services, including helping you keep your home or sell it for the best offer.

● To communicate with you about our services and promotions.

● To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

● To comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

Sharing your information

We may share your personal information with the following third parties:

● Service providers who help us provide our services, such as website hosting, payment processing, and email delivery.

● Government authorities or law enforcement officials if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

● Third-party marketing partners with your consent.

Your choices

You can control certain types of information we collect through your browser settings or by disabling cookies. You can also choose to unsubscribe from our marketing communications at any time.
